Intro

Hi there! My name is Atul Parida and I'm a software developer based in India. With a user-focused creative mindset and a passion for learning, innovation, and development, I love creating solutions that make people's lives easier. Currently, I'm studying as a sophomore at the Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD).

The fields in which I have had experience in include:

  • Web development through popular libraries
  • Mobile app development in both iOS and Android
  • Database management, both locally and server-hosted
  • Software testing and technical documentation

    Over the years I have gained mastery in web development, primarily focusing on JavaScript-based frameworks like React and Vue.js. I have also worked with PHP and Flask in some projects.

    Backend Development

    My experience in backend development has mostly involved SQL-based databases and NoSQL Firebase implementations.

    Mobile App Development

    Most of my mobile app development experience has been in iOS, but I have also worked with Android and cross-platform tools like Xamarin and Flutter.

    • Spacemate

      Spacemate is a space-management system that I developed with a team of 5 members using Android Studio and Firebase for our 50.001 Information Systems & Programming course. It allows students to book rooms and facilities throughout the campus, helping everyone find study locations more efficiently.

    • Flask-Based Mathematics Website

      Using Flask, Python and Transcrypt, my team of two members developed a mathematics quiz website as part of our 10.020 Data Driven World course. It allows students to log in and access mathematics questions posed to them by other students & faculty. The website also features a leaderboard and an admin panel for faculty to manage the questions.

    • Python-Based Quiz Game

      Using Python, my five-member team developed a quiz game as part of our 10.014 Computational Thinking & Design course. Our restriction was to develop it purely in Python, without any GUI libraries. The game features a leaderboard and a variety of questions, and is played in a rudimentary interface, with bosses, multipliers and more.

    • Single-Frame Chatbot App

      Using Swift, my team of 2 members developed a single-frame chatbot template for the DaVinci@SUTD STEAMxD 2023 workshop series, intended to teach Singaporean JC and Polytechnic students basic mobile app development. The chatbot is able to answer based on a list of responses in a text-bubble format. Additional functionalities such as API calls to OpenAI GPT-3 and button layouts are also provided for students to explore.

    • Plane Spotting Telegram Bot

      Using Python, I developed a Telegram bot that provides users the details of planes flying in their area, retrieved using the OpenSky Network API. At the moment, the Telegram bot operates only for airports in and around Singapore, but I plan to expand it to other countries as well. This bot is in development but will be hosted using Heroku.
